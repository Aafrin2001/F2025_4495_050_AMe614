EAi Healthcare Companion App - User Guide

Welcome to EAi Healthcare Companion
====================================

EAi (Enabled AI) Healthcare Companion is a comprehensive mobile application designed specifically for seniors and their caregivers. This user-friendly app helps you manage your health, track daily activities, communicate with AI assistance, and maintain your independence while staying connected to your healthcare needs.

1. Getting Started
==================

What You'll Need
----------------
- A smartphone or tablet (iOS or Android)
- Internet connection
- Email address for account creation
- Basic familiarity with touchscreen devices

Downloading the App
------------------
1. Visit the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
2. Search for "EAi Healthcare Companion"
3. Tap "Install" or "Get"
4. Wait for the download to complete
5. Tap "Open" to launch the app

First Launch
------------
When you first open the app, you'll see:
- Welcome splash screen with the EAi logo
- Brief introduction to the app's features
- Option to create a new account or sign in

2. Account Setup and Login
=========================

Creating Your Account
---------------------
1. Tap "Create Account" on the welcome screen
2. Fill in your information:
   - First Name: Your given name
   - Last Name: Your family name
   - Email Address: A valid email you check regularly
   - Phone Number: Optional, but helpful for reminders
   - Password: Choose a secure password (at least 6 characters)
   - Confirm Password: Re-enter your password
3. Select your user type:
   - "A senior looking for health support" - For seniors using the app themselves
   - "A caregiver or family member" - For family members or caregivers
4. Tap "Create Account"
5. You'll receive a confirmation message

Signing In
----------
1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Tap "Sign In"
4. You'll be taken to the main dashboard

Forgot Password
---------------
1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

3. Main Dashboard Overview
==========================

The main dashboard is your central hub for accessing all app features. Here's what you'll see:

Top Navigation
--------------
- EAi Logo: Tap to return to dashboard
- Settings Icon: Access app settings
- Logout Button: Sign out of your account

Welcome Card
------------
- Personalized greeting with your name
- "AI-powered health companion" subtitle
- Quick overview of your daily progress

Feature Cards
-------------
The dashboard displays four main feature cards:

1. Monitor Card (Heart Icon)
   - Track your health metrics
   - View health records and trends
   - Record vital signs

2. Activities Card (Fitness Icon)
   - Access health activities and games
   - Track your daily exercise
   - Play cognitive games

3. Medication Card (Medical Icon)
   - Manage your medications
   - Set up reminders

4. Dashboard Card (Analytics Icon)
   - View your health summary
   - See progress reports
   - Access detailed analytics

Chat with AI Button
------------------
- Large, prominent button at the bottom
- Quick access to AI assistant
- Available for health questions and support

4. Health Monitoring Features
=============================

Recording Health Data
---------------------
1. Tap the "Monitor" card on the main dashboard
2. Tap "Add New Record"
3. Select the type of measurement:
   - Blood Pressure (systolic/diastolic)
   - Heart Rate (beats per minute)
   - Weight (pounds or kilograms)
   - Temperature (Fahrenheit or Celsius)
   - Blood Sugar (mg/dL)
   - Sleep Hours
   - Mood (1-10 scale)
   - Pain Level (1-10 scale)
4. Enter your measurement value
5. Add any notes (optional)
6. Tap "Save"

Viewing Health Records
---------------------
1. From the Health Monitoring screen, tap "View Records"
2. You'll see a list of all your health measurements
3. Records are sorted by date (newest first)
4. Tap any record to view details or edit

Health Summary
--------------
1. Tap "Health Summary" to see:
   - Average values for each health metric
   - Trends over time (7, 30, or 90 days)
   - Charts showing your progress
   - Recommendations based on your data

Setting Health Goals
--------------------
1. In the Health Monitoring section, tap "Goals"
2. Set target values for:
   - Blood pressure ranges
   - Weight goals
   - Exercise minutes per day
   - Sleep hours per night
3. The app will track your progress toward these goals

5. Activity Tracking and Games
===============================

Health Activities
-----------------
Access the Activities section to find:

Morning Walk
- Tap to start a 15-minute walking session
- Timer will count down your walk
- Earn 10 points upon completion
- Track your daily steps

Stretching Exercises
- 10-minute stretching routine
- Step-by-step guided instructions
- Earn 5 points for completion
- Improves flexibility and mobility

Deep Breathing
- 5-minute breathing exercise
- Guided breathing patterns
- Earn 5 points for completion
- Reduces stress and anxiety

Sleep Cycle Tracking
- Record your sleep hours
- Track sleep quality
- Earn 15 points for completion
- Monitor sleep patterns

Adding Custom Activities
-----------------------
1. Tap the "+" button in the Activities section
2. Enter activity name (e.g., "Evening Stroll")
3. Add description (optional)
4. Set duration in minutes
5. Tap "Add Activity"
6. Your custom activity will appear in the list

Cognitive Games
---------------
Memory Match
- Match pairs of cards to improve memory
- Three difficulty levels
- Track your best scores
- Earn points based on performance

Word Search
- Find hidden words in letter grids
- Health and wellness themed puzzles
- Progressive difficulty
- Improves cognitive function

Puzzle Pieces
- Complete jigsaw puzzles
- Various difficulty levels
- Relaxing and engaging
- Enhances problem-solving skills

Number Sequence
- Complete number patterns
- Mathematical reasoning exercises
- Adaptive difficulty
- Improves logical thinking

Tracking Progress
-----------------
- View your activity completion rate
- See total points earned
- Track streaks for consistent activities
- View weekly and monthly summaries

6. AI Chat Assistant
====================

Accessing AI Chat
-----------------
1. Tap "Chat with AI" on the main dashboard
2. Choose between:
   - Text Chat: Type your questions
   - Voice Chat: Speak your questions

Text Chat Features
------------------
1. Type your health-related questions
2. The AI will provide helpful responses
3. Use quick action buttons for common queries:
   - "Check my vitals"
   - "Medication reminder"
   - "Schedule appointment"
   - "Emergency help"

Voice Chat Features
-------------------
1. Tap the microphone button
2. Speak clearly into your device
3. The AI will process your speech
4. Listen to the AI's response
5. Tap the microphone again to stop

Common AI Interactions
----------------------
Ask questions like:
- "What's my blood pressure trend?"
- "When should I take my medication?"
- "How can I improve my sleep?"
- "What exercises are good for seniors?"
- "I'm feeling dizzy, what should I do?"

Emergency Situations
--------------------
- For medical emergencies, call 911 immediately
- The AI can provide general health guidance
- Always consult healthcare professionals for serious concerns
- The app is not a substitute for professional medical care

7. Medication Management
========================

Adding Medications
------------------
1. Tap the "Medication" card on the dashboard
2. Tap "Add Medication"
3. Enter medication details:
   - Medication name
   - Dosage (e.g., "10mg", "1 tablet")
   - Frequency (e.g., "Twice daily", "Every 8 hours")
   - Instructions (e.g., "Take with food")
   - Start date
   - End date (if applicable)
4. Tap "Save"

Viewing Medication List
-----------------------
1. From the Medication section, tap "My Medications"
2. See all your current medications
3. View medication history
4. Check adherence rates

Setting Reminders
-----------------
1. Tap on any medication in your list
2. Tap "Set Reminder"
3. Choose reminder times
4. Enable notifications
5. The app will remind you when it's time to take medication

Medication Adherence Tracking
-----------------------------
- The app tracks when you mark medications as taken
- View adherence reports
- See patterns in your medication routine
- Get alerts for missed doses

9. Troubleshooting
==================

Common Issues and Solutions
---------------------------

App Won't Open
--------------
- Restart your device
- Check if you have the latest app version
- Ensure you have sufficient storage space
- Try uninstalling and reinstalling the app

Login Problems
--------------
- Verify your email and password
- Check your internet connection
- Try the "Forgot Password" option
- Contact support if issues persist

Data Not Syncing
----------------
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you're signed in to your account
- Try logging out and back in
- Restart the app

Notifications Not Working
------------------------
- Check your device's notification settings
- Ensure the app has permission to send notifications
- Verify notification preferences in app settings
- Restart your device

Performance Issues
------------------
- Close other apps to free up memory
- Restart the app
- Update to the latest version
- Restart your device

Getting Technical Support
------------------------
- Use the "Help" section in the app
- Contact support through the app settings
- Email support@eaihealthcare.com
- Call the support hotline: 1-800-EAI-HELP
